## Product Launch Platforms

### Product Hunt
- **Launch day** - Prepare GIFs, screenshots, clear value prop
- **Tagline**: "Compare 10 bridge protocols, optimize costs by 65%"
- **First comment** - Founder story, why you built this
- **Engage** - Reply to every comment within 1 hour
- **Hunter** - Ask someone with followers to hunt it

### Hacker News (news.ycombinator.com)
- **Show HN**: "Show HN: Bridge Aggregator API - Compare 10 Protocols"
- **Best time**: Weekday mornings (7-9am PST)
- **First comment**: Tech details, open source parts, performance numbers
- **Engage**: Answer technical questions immediately

### BetaList
- **Submit**: https://betalist.com/submit
- **Focus**: Early adopter developers

### AlternativeTo
- **List as alternative** to: Across, Hop, Socket, Li.Fi
- **Highlight**: Aggregation, cost savings, reliability scoring
